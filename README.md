# customer_behavior_analysis
This project analyzes 3,900 transactions using Python, SQL, and Power BI to uncover shopping behaviors. Insights on spending patterns and subscriptions guide strategic decisions like targeted marketing, optimizing discounts, and boosting loyalty.

Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis📌 Project Overview:
This project analyzes customer shopping behavior using transactional data from 3,900 purchases across various product categories. The primary goal is to uncover actionable insights into spending patterns, customer segments, product preferences, and subscription behavior to guide strategic business decisions.

📊 Dataset SummarySize: 
3,900 Rows, 18 Columns.Customer Demographics: Age, Gender, Location, Subscription Status.Purchase Details: Item Purchased, Category, Purchase Amount, Season, Size, Color.Shopping Behavior: Discount Applied, Frequency of Purchases, Review Rating, Shipping Type.

🛠️ Tech Stack & Workflow:
This project demonstrates an end-to-end data analysis workflow, utilizing the following tools:
1. Data Cleaning & Exploratory Data Analysis (Python)Library Used: Pandas.Missing Data Handling: Imputed missing values in the Review Rating column using the median rating of each product category.Data Standardization: Renamed columns to snake_case for better readability.Feature Engineering: Created new columns, including age_group (by binning customer ages) and purchase_frequency_days.
   
2. Business Transaction Analysis (PostgreSQL): The cleaned dataset was loaded into a PostgreSQL database to answer key business questions:Revenue Analysis: Compared total revenue generated by male vs. female customers and calculated revenue contribution by age group.
   
3. Customer Segmentation: Classified customers into New, Returning, and Loyal segments based on purchase history.Product Performance: Identified the top 5 products by rating and the top 3 products per category.Subscription Value: Compared average spend and total revenue across subscription statuses.
   
4. Data Visualization (Power BI): An interactive Customer Behavior Dashboard was developed in Power BI to present the findings visually. The dashboard tracks:Average Purchase Amount and Review Ratings.Percentage of Customers by Subscription Status.Revenue and Sales broken down by Category and Age Group.
   
💡Key Business Recommendations:
Based on the analysis, the following strategic actions are recommended:
1. Boost Subscriptions: Promote exclusive benefits for subscribers to increase recurring revenue.Customer.
2. Loyalty Programs: Reward repeat buyers to move them into the high-value "Loyal" segment.
3. Review Discount Policy: Balance sales boosts with careful margin control, especially for highly discount-dependent products.
4. Targeted Marketing: Focus marketing efforts on high-revenue age groups and customers utilizing express shipping.
